TOKENOMICS

PIGC has a buy tax of 5%, where 4% goes to the LP, 1.5% goes for continued development, and 0.5% goes to the holders, and a sell tax of 8%, where 6% goes to the LP, 1% for continued development, and 1% to the holders.

Like the original PIG’s community, we do not believe in team allocations, presales, or VC seed funding since these are anti-decentralization. And since PIGC is fully community-owned, it’s been stealth launched to be fair for everyone, with all the circulating supply listed on PancakeSwap. 50% of the supply has also been burned to create the black hole that will exponentially reduce supply.

THE COMMUNITY BENEFITS MOST:

No Presale

Without a presale or a whitelist, no one receives an unfair advantage by buying at preferential under-the-market prices before the launch.

Fully Decentralized

The community owns the token, not a team or a dev, which is why there are no team tokens and all of the circulating supply is listed on PancakeSwap.

Rising Liquidity

With every transaction, a whopping 4% to 6% goes to the LP depending on whether it’s a sell or a buy. This not only increases the BNB in the pool, it increases the floor price!
